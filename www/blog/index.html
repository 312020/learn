    • 16-Jun, 2015A change to the way our URLs work

      We've just landed a change to the way JS Bin URLs work, and though the new way aims to be intuitive and backward compatible, some of you sharp sighted users might have spotted the change already and wondering what changed and why. Revisions are referred to as "snapshots". Snapshots (the numerical identifier) only appear in the URL when take a snapshot (and want to share that snapshot).
